The Goal of 3R

Preserving the environment and reducing waste accumulation that can pollute ecosystems and cause harm to both humans and the environment.

Let's Go!

Reduce

Minimizing the use of items that could potentially become waste.

Examples: reducing the use of plastic bags, Styrofoam, plastic bottles, disposable food containers, and single-sided paper.

Reuse

Reusing items that can still be utilized.

Examples: using reusable shopping bags, food containers, water bottles, or repurposing old clothes as cleaning cloths.

Recycle

Transforming waste into new usable items.

Examples: converting organic waste into compost, using old plastic bottles as plant pots, and more.

Issues in Indonesia

Based on Sanbear Tech Innovators research, we found that!

  1. Indonesia ranks as the second-largest producer of plastic waste in the world after China, according to the United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP).
  2. Additionally, data from Indonesia's Ministry of Environment and Forestry indicates that in 2023, the country generated 69.9 million tons of waste, with food waste accounting for 41.60%, plastic waste 18.71%, and household waste around 44.37%.
  3. According to data reported by Liputan6.com, nearly 80% of the Bantar Gebang area is covered by waste piles. Tragically, this area remains a residential zone where locals live and earn a livelihood by scavenging.
  4. This situation has significantly impacted the health of nearby communities, particularly in 2023, when air pollution levels rose due to the stench from garbage piles stretching up to 1.5 km from the site. This caused numerous children to suffer from respiratory infections (ISPA).
  5. The total waste accumulation for 2024 is projected to be 31.9 million tons.

Solution

Sanbear Tech Innovators concluded 2 solutions to become a key that help solve the global problems.

  • Reuse means using something again instead of throwing it away. For example, instead of tossing out a glass jar, you can clean it and use it to store food or small items. Reusing helps reduce waste because it gives items a second life without needing to make or buy new ones.

  • Recycle means turning old or used items into something new. For instance, plastic bottles can be collected, melted down, and turned into new plastic products. Recycling helps reduce the amount of waste in landfills and saves resources by reusing materials to make new things.

Innovation

Based on Sanbear Tech's innovators solution, we found a great innovation that could be done as students and citizens worldwide to help our country and overcome problems globally

  1. Recyper
    2. Recyper stands for "recycle paper," which is the process of turning used paper into new sheets of paper.
    Steps :
    1. Collect used paper.
    2. Cut the paper into small pieces and place them in a blender.
    3. Blend the paper until smooth, then add water and blend again to create a paper pulp. Add color if desired, and blend again until evenly mixed.
    4. Mix the paper pulp with water in a basin and stir well.
    5. Dip a paper screen into the basin, ensuring an even layer of pulp covers the screen, and then gently lift it out.
    6. Use a sponge to press the wet paper sheet onto a towel to remove excess water.
    7. Once the paper sheet releases from the screen, let it dry for at least overnight. The finished paper is now ready to use.
